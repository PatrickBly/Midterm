My Favorite Foods

List of my top favorite foods.

  1. General Tso's Chicken
  2. Sushi
  3. Fried Cod
  4. Orange Chicken
  5. Naan Pizza

General Tso's Chicken

Normally, I don't like Chicken because it is tough and hard to chew if it's grilled, but the way general Tso's is made, it's easy to chew.

General Tso's originates from a Chinese general but is used the most in American cooking.

Sushi

Sushi can contain many foods inside of it. This gives it potential to be both very good and very bad. The majority of the time you get to choose whats in it so you end up with a good sushi roll.

Sushi originates from China and was first created between the 3rd and 5th centuries BC.

Fried Cod

As opposed to grilled meats like chicken and steak, fish is easier to chew and when paired with Tartar sauce, tastes better.

Spain and Portugal started to fry fish in the 16th century making them the inventors of the meal.

Orange Chicken

Orange Chicken is just like General Tso's but it has a sweeter sauce. The sauce tastes good but I would not rate it as high as General Tso's.

Orange chicken was originated by a chef in the Hunan Province in China

Naan Pizza

The Pizza is easy to make and overflows with grease. It's not healthy in any way but tastes very good.

Naan bread is originally from western Asia. Naan being used in pizza doesn't have any clear origin.
